Classic Pasta Salad

This pasta salad is refreshing and versatile, perfect for picnics or as a side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces pasta (such as rotini or fusilli)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Italian salad dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine pasta, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, and parsley.
  3. Add Italian dressing and toss until evenly coated.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
  5. Optional: Add grilled chicken or shrimp for a heartier dish.
